Outlaw Economy is a complete economy suite for Paper and Spigot 1.20.4 servers. It combines secure player balances, NPC-driven shops, a global marketplace, and a polished trading system so communities can manage money without third-party dependencies.

Key Features
------------
- Persistent player balances with customizable starting funds and currency formatting.
- NPC shop templates for buying and selling items, including price multipliers and live adjustments.
- General marketplace where players list items for sale with built-in price caps to prevent inflation.
- Drag-and-drop selling flow that protects players from losing items when their price is rejected.
- Player-to-player trade UI supporting shared inventories, configurable money offers, and confirmation safeguards.
- Rapid profit alerts that notify staff when someone earns 20k within five minutes.
- Vault bridge and lightweight API for seamless integration with other plugins.

Why Choose Outlaw Economy?
---------------------------
Outlaw Economy was built for survival and faction servers that want responsive tools without sacrificing control. Administrators can fine-tune shops and balances, staff receive real-time alerts, and players enjoy intuitive menus that keep transactions safe.

Whether you need a plug-and-play economy or a foundation for custom systems, Outlaw Economy keeps your server's finances organized, transparent, and fun.
